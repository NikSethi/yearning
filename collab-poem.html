twentysomethings collab poems

in august of 2021, some of my friends and i decided to make collaborative poems. each of us would start with a skeleton and hand it off to the next person to write an iteration. by the end, we'd have 4 versions of 4 poems.

we had the prompt:

With each revision, make a copy in this document before adding/editing so we can see the progression! Feel free to add / subtract as much as you’d like — perhaps only a line survives.

here they are:

  • untitled
  • life story
  • my roommate’s been going back into the office
  • five years
